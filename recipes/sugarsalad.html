Sugar Salad

Description

A sugar snap pea salad is a vibrant, refreshing dish featuring sweet and crunchy sugar snap peas,
often combined with other vegetables like cucumbers, bell peppers, and onions, and sometimes fruits like mandarin oranges.

It's typically tossed in a flavorful dressing, often a sesame-soy dressing. The salad's appeal lies
in its combination of textures (crunchy peas, soft vegetables) and sweet, savory, and tangy flavors.
